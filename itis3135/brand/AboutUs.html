I'm Mytien Hoang, the creator of Hoang Kong Boba and a friend to all! It's a pleasure to introduce myself as well as give a brief background about the company. As you already Hoang Kong Boba is a local boba shop in Charlotte NC that serves Hong Kong style teas. Though there are many types, I wanted to bring my favorite style of Boba to your Charlotte area!

Hong Kong Milk Teas are rich in flavor and tend to give that Tea taste when consumed. The style of tea is very caffinated and strong but very delicious. Though we have many choices to choose from, we suggest our classic teas and then work around to our more "fancy" ones.
