Link to a Well Done Website

Paperlike.com is the official website for the product Paperlike. The website is used to advertise, inform, and sell the product. the Website uses the properties of CRAP very well and it's visual design is pleasing to the eye. In the bullet points below, I will analyze how well they use the properties of CRAP.

  • Contrast: Using different font sizes, font styles and font types, headings and sub headings contrast greatly. This makes it easier to read and capture information
  • Repetition: The theming of the website is simple and repetive. They constantly use a theme of colors and formating style in every page.
  • Alignment: Alignment of all the text is correctly formatted, repeated throughout the webpage, and pleasing to the eye.
  • Proximity: All images that need to be shown can be seen closely to the text. The website uses negative space well and even with the change in web-browser size, everything still aligns greatly.
Link to a Poorly Done Website

This is the official website for Yale School of Art Graduate school . The website is used to inform current students about events within the school as well as advertise the Master's programs that they offer. The website presents the art pieces that the students have done. The visual design of the website itself is poor. The background is not large enough to fit the size of a web browser and the text does not auto align when the web-browser changes size. There is little contrast and the lack of theme makes the website hard to look at. In the bullet points below, I will explain the lack of CRAP qualities in this website.

  • Contrast: There is a major lack of contrast. The background color is too bright and clashes with the color of text. Rather then changing the background, they changed the highlight text which is distracting and creates a bigger problem.
  • Repetition: There is minimal repetition as the images do not follow a same format. There is small theme in color and the text changes from having margins to not having any at all. The theme colors clash with eachother making it hard to read.
  • Alignment: There is no alignment in the text. There is no auto alignment or self alignment which makes it hard to read.
  • Proximity:The use of images are poor because of the lack of self alignment. The margins don't match with all of the images displayed.
